# Contributing

Contributions are welcome if they improve reusable cognitive neuroscience mentoring workflows.

## Good Contributions

- Add method-specific references, such as fMRI, fNIRS, MEG, eye-tracking, or computational modeling guidance.
- Add better examples for result interpretation, literature synthesis, manuscript writing, or reviewer responses.
- Improve ethical boundaries for child, clinical, or education-risk research.
- Clarify workflows without adding private project data.

## Rules

- Do not add participant data, manuscripts, PDFs, analysis results, or private local paths.
- Do not add fabricated citations or unsupported claims.
- Keep `SKILL.md` concise. Put detailed guidance in `references/`.
- Prefer reusable workflows over project-specific instructions.

## Suggested Review Checklist

- Does the change generalize beyond one user's project?
- Does it preserve claim/evidence/boundary discipline?
- Does it avoid overclaiming neural or clinical interpretation?
- Does it keep private data out of the repository?
